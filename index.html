Broom of the Witching Hour

When I was six years old, in a quiet town surrounded by the back of our house, I experienced a chilling encounter that still sends shivers down my spine. It was an ordinary morning, the world bathed in pre-dawn shadows. I woke up unusually early, the unsettling feeling that something wasn't right lingering in the air.

As I lay in bed, straining my ears, an eerie sound reached me—a rhythmic sweeping, echoing from the backyard. Confused and curious, I crept out of my room, following the haunting noise. The moonlight revealed a cloaked figure, hunched over and sweeping the yard with an old broom.

Fear gripped me as I watched the mysterious sweeper. The figure, hidden in shadows, seemed to sense my presence. The sweeping stopped, and the figure turned toward me, a bony hand pointing in my direction. Paralyzed, I could only watch as the sweeping resumed, the figure fading into the night.

Retreating to my room, I pulled the covers tight, haunted by the memory. The backyard, once a place of joy, now held a lingering unease. The rhythmic sweeping, the pointed finger—it was a horror etched into my childhood, a mysterious visitor in the silent hours before dawn.

The Phantom Hourglass: A Decade of Midnight Terrors - My Ten-Year-Old Encounter with Sleep Paralysis

When I was just ten, a peculiar night unfolded in my bedroom. I awoke to find myself stuck in sleep paralysis. The moon's gentle glow illuminated the room, setting the stage for a disquieting episode.

Unable to move, I felt an unusual weight on my chest, as if an invisible force held me down. In the faint light, a shadowy figure materialized, advancing towards me. Strange whispers filled the air, adding to the sense of unease.

As the figure loomed, the paralysis gradually released its hold. With effort, I regained control, and the spectral presence faded away. Alone in the moonlit room, I grappled with the blurred line between dreams and reality, left with a lasting impression of that unsettling night. The memory lingered, a reminder of the mysterious realms that come alive when the night falls silent.
